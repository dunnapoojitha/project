Understanding Moderate Air Pollution

Moderate air pollution means the air quality is acceptable; however, some pollutants may pose a risk for sensitive individuals like children, the elderly, or those with respiratory conditions. Monitoring AQI and minimizing outdoor exposure can help avoid health risks.

Air Quality Index (AQI) Levels

Green Good 0 - 50 Air quality is satisfactory, and air pollution poses little or no risk.
Yellow Moderate 51 - 100 Air quality is acceptable. However, there may be a risk for some people who are unusually sensitive.
Orange Unhealthy for Sensitive Groups 101 - 150 Sensitive groups may experience effects. Public not likely affected.
Red Unhealthy 151 - 200 Some of the general public may experience health effects.
Purple Very Unhealthy 201 - 300 Health alert: everyone may experience health effects.
Maroon Hazardous 301 and higher Health warning: everyone is more likely to be affected.
