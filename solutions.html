Let's fight air pollution

Tree Plantation

Trees naturally filter air, absorb carbon dioxide, and release oxygen. Community tree planting improves air quality and biodiversity.

Solar Power

Solar panels reduce dependence on fossil fuels. Solar homes and street lights cut emissions and promote renewable living.

Electric Vehicles

EVs eliminate tailpipe pollution, reduce noise, and improve urban air quality. Governments offer subsidies for switching to EVs.

Awareness Programs

Awareness campaigns educate people about pollution sources and eco-friendly actions. Change starts with informed citizens.

Use of Public Transport

Buses, metros, and carpools reduce the number of vehicles on roads, cutting fuel use and lowering smog levels.

Smart Air Purifiers

Indoor air pollution is deadly too. Installing air purifiers in schools, hospitals, and homes ensures safer breathing indoors.

Biogas Usage

Converting organic waste to biogas reduces landfill emissions and replaces polluting fuels with cleaner cooking gas.

Air Quality Monitoring

Sensors track pollution levels in real-time. Mobile apps help people avoid hotspots and demand action from authorities.
